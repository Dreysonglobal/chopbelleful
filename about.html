About ChopBelleful

A restaurant, lounge & bar in Agege, Lagos — Wine. Dine. Celebrate. Learn.

Our Story

ChopBelleful Wine & Dine is built on one simple idea: great food should feel like home, and every gathering should feel like a celebration. From our cozy lounge to our event catering and culinary training, we bring warmth, quality, and unforgettable taste to everything we do.

Whether you are stopping by for a relaxed meal, planning an event that deserves a standout menu, stocking up on premium frozen items, or learning to cook like a pro, you will find the same standard across the board: clean prep, bold flavors, and service that truly cares.

Our Mission

To create memorable food experiences through excellent service, quality ingredients, and a welcoming atmosphere.

What We Stand For

Consistency, hospitality, hygiene, and the joy of sharing meals with people you love.
